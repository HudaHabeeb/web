KMCT Institute of Technology and Management

KMCT Institute of Technology and Management,Kuttipuram,, located in Kerala, is a renowned educational institution.

The institute offers a wide range of Degree programmes at UG level.

These courses are offered to students in Full Time mode across various fields, including Engineering.

One of the most popular programme offered at the institute is B.E. / B.Tech.

The institute strive to provide quality education to students at an affordable fee to make it accessible for meritorious students.

The institute has a group of experienced and professional faculty members to offer valuable education to students.
